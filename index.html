scPOEM: scRNA-seq and scATAC-seq paired Embedding Database

Overview: This database presents an integrated analysis of paired scRNA-seq and scATAC-seq data by scPOEM, which generates a joint low-dimensional embedding of genes and peaks for each cell type. Specifically, after quality control and the selection of 3,000 highly variable genes (HVGs), heterogeneous regulatory networks, including gene‚Äìgene, peak‚Äìpeak, and gene‚Äìpeak interactions, were inferred using LASSO, random forest, and XGBoost, and are then jointly embedded into a low-dimensional latent space using the scPOEM R package.
